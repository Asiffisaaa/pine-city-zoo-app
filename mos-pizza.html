MOS PIZZA

Grab a delicious, authentic Italian meal at Mo's Pizza. They serve the most delicious thick-crust pizzas and delicious pastas.

WHAT CAN YOU EAT?

Mo's Pizza offers a wide variety of pizzas, pastas, and salads. You can choose from classic Margherita pizza to gourmet options like BBQ chicken or vegetarian delights. Their pasta dishes are equally tempting, with options like creamy Alfredo or spicy arrabbiata. Don't forget to try their fresh salads and desserts!

WHAT IS THE ATMOSPHERE LIKE?

The atmosphere at Mo's Pizza is casual and family-friendly. The restaurant is decorated with a fun, vibrant theme that makes it a great place to relax after exploring the zoo. Whether you're dining in or taking out, you'll enjoy the lively ambiance.

WHAT MAKES IT SPECIAL?

Mo's Pizza is known for its commitment to quality ingredients and authentic recipes. They use fresh produce and traditional methods to create their dishes, ensuring that every bite is packed with flavor. Plus, it's conveniently located near the Monkey Trail, making it a perfect stop during your zoo visit!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND it?

You will find Mo's Pizza at the main food court.
