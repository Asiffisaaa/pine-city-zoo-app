LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a unique exhibit that recreates the lush, vibrant ecosystem of a tropical rainforest. Visitors can explore winding paths and discover a variety of plant and animal species that thrive in this rich environment.

Picnic tables and bathrooms are provided for visitor convenience.

The Lost Forest is a fascinating place to learn about the world of tropical ecosystems. You can see everything from exotic birds and butterflies to monkeys and sloths. The exhibits are designed to be both educational and entertaining, making it a great stop for visitors of all ages.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND it?

The Lost Forest is at the eastern side of the zoo, beyond the Monkey Trail.
