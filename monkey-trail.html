ANIMALS MAPS PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Whether you choose to walk or ride, you will find the monkey trail very challenging. Take the 30 minute or 1 hour trail.

The Monkey Trail is a fascinating place to learn about the world of primates. You can see everything from playful capuchins to majestic orangutans. The exhibits are designed to be both educational and entertaining, making it a great stop for visitors of all ages.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND it?

The Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo, next to the Insect House.
